Research prompts

Step 2 — use these prompts to gather audience, brand, and category evidence

How to use this guide

Each prompt is designed to be flexible. Paste it directly into your AI tool — the model will ask you the right questions before proceeding. Replace placeholder text in brackets with your specific details when prompted. Prompts work best with Claude, ChatGPT-4o, or equivalent.

ChatGPT deep research

Audience & category deep research
Use when you need substantive background on fans, behaviours, or the broader category
ChatGPT deep research
How to use
  • Identify an angle to explore — e.g. why South Africans are passionate about soccer, or how fans of [team] engage digitally
  • Ask ChatGPT to write a prompt for deep research on that angle — tell it to stick to research, not insight generation
  • Run that prompt using the Deep Research function in ChatGPT
  • Ask ChatGPT to summarise the findings and share relevant sources
Starter prompt
Write me a deep research prompt to explore [insert angle — e.g. why fans of [sport/team] engage the way they do]. The prompt should focus purely on gathering research and data — no insight generation or recommendations. It should be structured to get credible, sourced findings.

GROK — social media listening

Social listening via GROK
Use to understand what fans are actually saying on social media about a brand, team, or topic
GROK
How to use
  • Ask ChatGPT to write a GROK prompt to conduct social listening on your chosen topic
  • Paste and run that prompt in GROK
  • Copy or download the GROK report back into ChatGPT
  • Ask for 5–10 key findings from the report
Starter prompt (paste into ChatGPT first)
Write me a GROK prompt to conduct social listening on [insert topic — e.g. how fans of [team/sport] talk about [brand/category] on social media]. The prompt should be designed to surface real fan sentiment, recurring themes, and notable conversations.

GWI — audience data

GWI data analysis via ChatGPT
Use to extract meaningful findings from GWI crosstab exports
GWI + ChatGPT
How to use
  • Download a crosstab export from GWI as an Excel file
  • Upload it to ChatGPT
  • Use one of the prompts below to extract findings
Prompt — general findings
Analyse this GWI crosstab data and share 5–10 key findings about the audience. Focus on behaviours, attitudes, and interests that stand out as significant or surprising.
Prompt — unusual interests
Based on this GWI data, what is something unusual or unexpected that this audience is interested in — something that wouldn't be obvious from their demographic profile alone?
Prompt — interests outside sport
What interests or behaviours does this audience have outside of sport? Identify the top 5 non-sport interests or passions that define this group.
Prompt — shared beliefs
What are the top 5 attitudes, beliefs, or behaviours that this audience shares most strongly? Focus on things that reveal how they see themselves or the world.

AI sources check

Credibility check for AI-generated research
Use to verify that findings from AI tools are reliable before building insights on them
Prompt
For each finding you've shared, tell me: what is the source or basis for this claim, how confident are you in its accuracy, and is there a reputable published study, report, or data set that supports it? Flag anything that is a generalisation or assumption rather than a verified fact.
